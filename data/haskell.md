# Top Books on Haskell

- **[Learn You a Haskell for Great Good!](http://learnyouahaskell.com/)** - *Miran Lipovaƒça* - *2011* - The most fun intro to Haskell and pure functional thinking; free online.
- **[Haskell Programming from First Principles](https://haskellbook.com/)** - *Christopher Allen & Julie Moronuki* - *2017* - A rigorous, beginner-friendly path through Haskell and type theory.
- **[Haskell for Imperative Programmers](https://leanpub.com/haskell-for-imperative-programmers)** - *Philipp Hagenlocher* - *2024* - Written for developers from OOP/imperative backgrounds; practical and grounded.
- **[Category Theory for Programmers](https://bartoszmilewski.com/2014/10/28/category-theory-for-programmers-the-preface/)** - *Bartosz Milewski* - *2019* - The mathematical foundations underlying functional programming; free online.
- **[Domain Modeling Made Functional](https://pragprog.com/titles/swdddf/domain-modeling-made-functional/)** - *Scott Wlaschin* - *2018* - DDD and functional design in F#; invaluable even if you never write F#.
