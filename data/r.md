# Top Books on R

- **[R for Data Science, 2nd Ed.](https://r4ds.hadley.nz/)** - *Hadley Wickham, Mine Çetinkaya-Rundel, Garrett Grolemund* - *2023* - Data science with R: import, transform, visualize, and model; free online.
- **[An Introduction to Statistical Learning with R](https://www.statlearning.com/)** - *James, Witten, Hastie, Tibshirani, Taylor* - *2021* - Statistical learning methods with R labs; free online.
- **[R in Action, 3rd Ed.](https://www.manning.com/books/r-in-action-third-edition)** - *Robert I. Kabacoff* - *2022* - Data analysis and graphics with R and Tidyverse; regression, hypothesis testing, time series.
- **[The Book of R](https://nostarch.com/bookofr)** - *Tilman M. Davies* - *2016* - A first course in R programming and statistics with hands-on exercises.
- **[Modern Statistics with R](http://www.modernstatisticswithr.com/)** - *Måns Thulin* - *2021* - Data wrangling, inference, predictive modeling, and simulation; free online.
