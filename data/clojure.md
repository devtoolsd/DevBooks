# Top Books on Clojure

- **[Programming Clojure, 4th Ed.](https://pragprog.com/titles/shcloj4/programming-clojure-fourth-edition/)** - *Alex Miller, Stuart Halloway, Aaron Bedra* - *2022* - Definitive Clojure guide by the core team; immutability, concurrency, tooling.
- **[The Joy of Clojure, 2nd Ed.](https://www.manning.com/books/the-joy-of-clojure-second-edition)** - *Michael Fogus, Chris Houser* - *2014* - Deep dive into Clojure's philosophy and idiomatic patterns.
- **[Getting Clojure](https://pragprog.com/titles/roclojure/getting-clojure/)** - *Russ Olsen* - *2018* - A clear, practical introduction to Clojure for developers.
- **[Elements of Clojure](https://www.amazon.com/Elements-Clojure-Zachary-Tellman/dp/1732880702)** - *Zachary Tellman* - *2019* - How to think in Clojure: naming, style, and design.
