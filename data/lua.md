# Top Books on Lua

- **[Programming in Lua, 4th Ed.](https://www.lua.org/pil/)** - *Roberto Ierusalimschy* - *2016* - The definitive Lua book by its chief architect; basics through the C API; free online.
- **[Lua Programming Gems](https://www.lua.org/gems/)** - *Lua community* - *2008* - Collection of articles on techniques, design, algorithms, and embedding Lua; free download.
- **[Beginning Lua Programming](https://www.amazon.com/Beginning-Lua-Programming-Kurt-Jung/dp/1590597521)** - *Kurt Jung & Aaron Brown* - *2007* - Practical Lua for databases, web, games, and C/Lua interfacing.
