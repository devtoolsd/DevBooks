# Top Books on Software Engineering

- **[The Pragmatic Programmer, 20th Anniversary Ed.](https://pragprog.com/titles/tpp20/the-pragmatic-programmer-20th-anniversary-edition/)** - *Andrew Hunt & David Thomas* - *2019* - Practical habits, thinking tools, and engineering instincts that age incredibly well.
- **[Code Complete, 2nd Ed.](https://www.microsoftpressstore.com/store/code-complete-9780735619678)** - *Steve McConnell* - *2004* - The "how to write good code" encyclopedia: construction, readability, and craft.
- **[Clean Code](https://www.amazon.com/Clean-Code-Handbook-Software-Craftsmanship/dp/0132350882)** - *Robert C. Martin* - *2008* - Principles and examples for writing code that stays readable under pressure.
- **[Clean Architecture](https://www.amazon.com/Clean-Architecture-Craftsmans-Software-Structure/dp/0134494164)** - *Robert C. Martin* - *2017* - Core architectural ideas for keeping systems maintainable as they grow.
- **[A Philosophy of Software Design, 2nd Ed.](https://www.amazon.com/Philosophy-Software-Design-John-Ousterhout/dp/1732102201)** - *John Ousterhout* - *2021* - A sharp counterpoint to Clean Code — argues for deep modules and against over-decomposition.
- **[Refactoring, 2nd Ed.](https://martinfowler.com/books/refactoring.html)** - *Martin Fowler* - *2018* - The classic playbook for improving design without changing behavior.
- **[Working Effectively with Legacy Code](https://www.amazon.com/Working-Effectively-Legacy-Michael-Feathers/dp/0131177052)** - *Michael Feathers* - *2004* - How to add tests and make changes safely in scary, untested codebases.
- **[Design Patterns](https://www.amazon.com/Design-Patterns-Elements-Reusable-Object-Oriented/dp/0201633612)** - *Gamma, Helm, Johnson, Vlissides* - *1994* - The OG patterns catalog — treat it as vocabulary, not dogma.
- **[Fundamentals of Software Architecture](https://fundamentalsofsoftwarearchitecture.com/)** - *Richards & Ford* - *2020* - Practical, modern guide to architecture styles, trade-offs, and the architect's role.
- **[The Mythical Man-Month](https://www.amazon.com/Mythical-Man-Month-Software-Engineering-Anniversary/dp/0201835959)** - *Frederick P. Brooks Jr.* - *1995* - Timeless lessons on why software schedules explode and what to do about it.
- **[The Clean Coder](https://www.amazon.com/Clean-Coder-Conduct-Professional-Programmers/dp/0137081073)** - *Robert C. Martin* - *2011* - Professionalism, estimates, pressure, and how to behave when things go wrong.
- **[Building Microservices, 2nd Ed.](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/building-microservices-2nd/9781492034018/)** - *Sam Newman* - *2021* - Designing and building microservices; evolution, integration, and deployment.
