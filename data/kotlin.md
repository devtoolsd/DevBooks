# Top Books on Kotlin

- **[Kotlin in Action, 2nd Ed.](https://www.manning.com/books/kotlin-in-action-second-edition)** - *Dmitry Jemerov & Svetlana Isakova* - *2019* - Practical Kotlin with the patterns you'll use on real projects.
- **[Effective Kotlin, 2nd Ed.](https://kt.academy/book/effectivekotlin)** - *Marcin Moskala* - *2022* - Best practices and idioms for writing clean, safe, maintainable Kotlin.
- **[Kotlin Programming: The Big Nerd Ranch Guide, 2nd Ed.](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/kotlin-programming-the/9780136870494/)** - *David Greenhalgh, Josh Skeen* - *2021* - From first principles to advanced Kotlin; hands-on examples.
- **[Programming Android with Kotlin](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/programming-android-with/9781492062998/)** - *Pierre-Olivier Laurence, Amanda Hinchman-Dominguez et al.* - *2021* - Concurrency, coroutines, and async in Kotlin for Android.
- **[Android Programming: The Big Nerd Ranch Guide, 5th Ed.](https://www.bignerdranch.com/books/android-programming-the-big-nerd-ranch-guide-5th-edition/)** - *Bill Phillips, Chris Stewart, Kristin Marsicano* - *2019* - Hands-on Android with Kotlin; coroutines and Jetpack Compose.
- **[Kotlin Cookbook](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/kotlin-cookbook/9781492041178/)** - *Ken Kousen* - *2019* - Practical recipes for common Kotlin tasks; concise and to the point.
- **[Head First Kotlin](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/head-first-kotlin/9781491996683/)** - *Dawn Griffiths, David Griffiths* - *2019* - Brain-friendly introduction to Kotlin; visual and example-driven.
