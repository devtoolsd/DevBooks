# Top Books on Pascal

- **[Pascal: An Introduction to the Art and Science of Programming](https://www.pearson.com/en-us/subject-catalog/p/Pascal-an-Introduction-to-the-Art-and-Science-of-Programming/P200000003249)** - *Walter J. Savitch* - *1995* - Classic CS1 textbook; abstraction and abstract data types.
- **[Software Tools in Pascal](https://www.amazon.com/Software-Tools-Pascal-Brian-Kernighan/dp/0201103427)** - *Brian W. Kernighan & P. J. Plauger* - *1981* - Good programming practice through complete, real-world programs in Pascal.
- **[Programming and Problem Solving with Pascal](https://www.amazon.com/Programming-Problem-Solving-Pascal-Sanford-Leestma/dp/0023694015)** - *Sanford Leestma & Larry Nyhoff* - *1993* - Structured programming, modular design, and algorithms with Pascal.
