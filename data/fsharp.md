# Top Books on F#

- **[F# in Action](https://www.manning.com/books/f-sharp-in-action)** - *Isaac Abraham* - *2024* - Comprehensive guide from foundational to advanced F# with practical applications.
- **[Domain Modeling Made Functional](https://pragprog.com/titles/swdddf/domain-modeling-made-functional/)** - *Scott Wlaschin* - *2018* - DDD and functional design in F#; invaluable for any language.
- **[The Book of F#](https://nostarch.com/f_sharp)** - *Dave Fancher* - *2014* - Practical F#: query expressions, type providers, parallel and async programming.
- **[F# Deep Dives](https://www.manning.com/books/f-sharp-deep-dives)** - *Tomas Petricek & Phillip Trelford (eds.)* - *2014* - Expert chapters on numerical computing, data viz, and business logic in F#.
