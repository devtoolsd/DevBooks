# Top Books on Angular

- **[Angular Development with TypeScript](https://www.manning.com/books/angular-development-with-typescript-second-edition)** - *Yakov Fain & Anton Moiseev* - *2018* - Angular with TypeScript; components, routing, forms, and HTTP.
- **[RxJS in Action](https://www.manning.com/books/rxjs-in-action)** - *Paul Daniels & Luis Atencio* - *2017* - Reactive programming with RxJS for Angular and beyond.
- **[Angular in Action](https://www.manning.com/books/angular-in-action)** - *Jeremy Wilken* - *2018* - Build real-world Angular applications; best practices and patterns.
- **[Angular Projects, 3rd Ed.](https://www.packtpub.com/product/angular-projects-third-edition/9781803239118)** - *Aristeidis Bampakos* - *2023* - Project-based Angular development with standalone components and modern features.
