# Top Books on OCaml

- **[Real World OCaml, 2nd Ed.](https://dev.realworldocaml.org/)** - *Yaron Minsky, Anil Madhavapeddy, Jason Hickey* - *2022* - Practical OCaml: data processing, systems, and web; free online.
- **[OCaml Programming: Correct + Efficient + Beautiful](https://cs3110.github.io/textbook/cover.html)** - *Michael R. Clarkson* - *2023* - Cornell textbook on FP and data structures in OCaml; free online.
- **[OCaml from the Very Beginning](https://johnwhitington.net/ocamlfromtheverybeginning/)** - *John Whitington* - *2013* - No-prerequisites introduction with small, progressive chapters.
- **[Functional Programming in OCaml](https://www.cs.cornell.edu/courses/cs3110/2021sp/)** - *Michael Clarkson* - *2021* - Course materials; semantics and software engineering with OCaml.
