# Top Books on Android

- **[Android Programming: The Big Nerd Ranch Guide, 5th Ed.](https://www.bignerdranch.com/books/android-programming-the-big-nerd-ranch-guide-5th-edition/)** - *Bryan Sills, Brian Gardner, Kristin Marsicano, Chris Stewart* - *2022* - Hands-on Android with Kotlin; coroutines and Jetpack Compose.
- **[Jetpack Compose by Tutorials](https://www.kodeco.com/books/jetpack-compose-by-tutorials)** - *Kodeco Team* - *2023* - Project-based guide to building modern Android UIs with Jetpack Compose.
- **[Kotlin in Action, 2nd Ed.](https://www.manning.com/books/kotlin-in-action-second-edition)** - *Dmitry Jemerov & Svetlana Isakova* - *2019* - Practical Kotlin for Android and server-side development.
- **[Programming Android with Kotlin](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/programming-android-with/9781492062998/)** - *Pierre-Olivier Laurence et al.* - *2021* - Concurrency, coroutines, and async in Kotlin for Android.
- **[Android Development with Kotlin](https://www.packtpub.com/product/android-development-with-kotlin-second-edition/9781801811583)** - *Marcin Moskala & Igor Wojda* - *2022* - Modern Android development with Kotlin and Jetpack.
