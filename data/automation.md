# Top Books on Automation

- **[Ansible for DevOps](https://www.ansiblefordevops.com/)** - *Jeff Geerling* - *2020* - Automate servers, cloud, and containers with Ansible; practical and hands-on.
- **[Pipeline as Code](https://www.manning.com/books/pipeline-as-code)** - *Mohamed Labouardy* - *2021* - CI/CD with Jenkins, Docker, Terraform; code-driven deployment pipelines.
- **[Grokking Continuous Delivery](https://www.manning.com/books/grokking-continuous-delivery)** - *Christie Wilson* - *2022* - Building effective CD pipelines and keeping software release-ready.
- **[CI/CD Design Patterns](https://www.packtpub.com/product/ci-cd-design-patterns/9781835889640)** - *Garima Bajpai et al.* - *2024* - Design patterns, pipeline components, testing, and deployment strategies.
