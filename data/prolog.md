# Top Books on Prolog

- **[The Art of Prolog, 2nd Ed.](https://mitpress.mit.edu/9780262691635/the-art-of-prolog)** - *Leon Sterling & Ehud Shapiro* - *1994* - Advanced Prolog techniques; the standard reference.
- **[Programming in Prolog: Using the ISO Standard](https://link.springer.com/book/10.1007/978-3-642-55481-0)** - *William Clocksin & Christopher Mellish* - *2003* - The classic Prolog textbook; ISO standard.
- **[The Craft of Prolog](https://mitpress.mit.edu/9780262512275/the-craft-of-prolog)** - *Richard O'Keefe* - *2009* - Using Prolog effectively; practical guidance beyond the basics.
- **[The Practice of Prolog](https://mitpress.mit.edu/9780262193016/the-practice-of-prolog)** - *Leon Sterling (ed.)* - *1990* - Designing and organizing larger Prolog programs; real-world projects.
