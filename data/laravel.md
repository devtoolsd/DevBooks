# Top Books on Laravel

- **[Laravel: Up & Running, 3rd Ed.](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/laravel-up/9781098153250/)** - *Matt Stauffer* - *2023* - The definitive practical guide to Laravel 10: Blade, Eloquent, testing, APIs.
- **[Laravel: From Apprentice To Artisan](https://leanpub.com/laravel)** - *Taylor Otwell* - *2023* - Architecture, dependency injection, and SOLID design in Laravel.
- **[Battle Ready Laravel](https://battlereadylaravel.com/)** - *Ash Allen* - *2023* - Testing, security, and production-ready Laravel applications.
- **[Test-Driven APIs with Laravel and Pest](https://www.packtpub.com/product/test-driven-apis-with-laravel-and-pest/9781803248011)** - *Paul Redmond* - *2023* - API development and testing with Laravel.
