# Top Books on AWS

- **[Amazon Web Services in Action, 3rd Ed.](https://www.manning.com/books/amazon-web-services-in-action-third-edition)** - *Andreas Wittig & Michael Wittig* - *2022* - In-depth guide to AWS: EC2, S3, Lambda, ECS, and more.
- **[AWS Cookbook](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/aws-cookbook/9781098148494/)** - *John Culkin & Mike Zazon* - *2023* - Practical recipes for IAM, networking, S3, and containerized services.
- **[Learning Amazon Web Services](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/learning-amazon-web/9780135298157/)** - *Mark Wilkins* - *2019* - Comprehensive guide to EC2, storage, Lambda, and infrastructure automation.
- **[AWS Security](https://www.manning.com/books/aws-security)** - *Dylan Shields* - *2023* - Identity, access management, data protection, logging, and monitoring on AWS.
