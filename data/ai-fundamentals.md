# Top Books on AI Fundamentals

- **[Artificial Intelligence: A Modern Approach, 4th Ed.](https://aima.cs.berkeley.edu/)** - *Russell & Norvig* - *2020* - The classic survey of AI: agents, search, logic, probability, and learning.
- **[Pattern Recognition and Machine Learning](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/publication/pattern-recognition-machine-learning/)** - *Christopher M. Bishop* - *2006* - Core probabilistic ML foundations with a Bayesian viewpoint — free online.
- **[The Elements of Statistical Learning, 2nd Ed.](https://hastie.su.domains/ElemStatLearn/)** - *Hastie, Tibshirani, Friedman* - *2009* - Deep statistical learning theory — a reference you'll keep returning to — free online.
- **[An Introduction to Statistical Learning, 2nd Ed.](https://www.statlearning.com/)** - *James, Witten, Hastie, Tibshirani, Taylor* - *2021* - Friendlier, applied companion to ESL — the best first stats-ML book — free online.
- **[Mathematics for Machine Learning](https://mml-book.github.io/)** - *Deisenroth, Faisal, Ong* - *2020* - Linear algebra, calculus, and probability as actually used in ML — free online.
