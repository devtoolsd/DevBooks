# Top Books on COBOL

- **[Beginning COBOL for Programmers](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/beginning-cobol-for/9781430262534/)** - *Michael Coughlan* - *2014* - COBOL for experienced programmers; ANS 85, file handling, OO COBOL, 100+ examples.
- **[Murach's Mainframe COBOL](https://www.murach.com/shop/murachs-mainframe-cobol-detail)** - *Mike Murach, Anne Prince, Raul Menendez* - *2018* - Mainframe COBOL: comprehensive coverage for professional use.
- **[COBOL for the 21st Century](https://www.wiley.com/en-us/COBOL+for+the+21st+Century%2C+11th+Edition-p-9780470462728)** - *Nancy Stern, Robert Stern, James Ley* - *2006* - Updated COBOL with modern practices and structure.
