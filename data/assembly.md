# Top Books on Assembly

- **[The Art of 64-Bit Assembly](https://nostarch.com/art-64-bit-assembly)** - *Randall Hyde* - *2022* - Modern x86-64 assembly with MASM; SIMD, strings, and machine organization.
- **[Assembly Language for x86 Processors, 8th Ed.](https://www.asmirvine.com/)** - *Kip Irvine* - *2020* - Classic textbook for Intel/Windows assembly; write and debug at the machine level.
- **[Modern X86 Assembly Language Programming](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/modern-x86-assembly/9781484296035/)** - *Daniel Kusswurm* - *2024* - x86-64 with AVX, AVX2, AVX-512; Windows and Linux; SIMD focus.
