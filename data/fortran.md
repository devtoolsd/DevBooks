# Top Books on Fortran

- **[Modern Fortran](https://www.manning.com/books/modern-fortran)** - *Milan Curcic* - *2020* - Building efficient parallel applications with modern Fortran.
- **[Modern Fortran Explained](https://global.oup.com/academic/product/modern-fortran-explained-9780198876571)** - *Michael Metcalf, John Reid, Malcolm Cohen* - *2018* - Authoritative reference for Fortran 2018; OOP and coarrays.
- **[Modern Fortran in Practice](https://www.cambridge.org/core/books/modern-fortran-in-practice/BC5BD23B2E478B4D457C5D6265BA9363)** - *Arjen Markus* - *2012* - Practical guidance on modern Fortran development and style.
