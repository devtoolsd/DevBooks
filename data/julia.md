# Top Books on Julia

- **[Practical Julia](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/practical-julia/9781098168544/)** - *Lee Phillips* - *2023* - Introduction to Julia: syntax, types, and applications in science and ML.
- **[Julia for Data Analysis](https://www.manning.com/books/julia-for-data-analysis)** - *Bogumił Kamiński* - *2022* - Data analysis and manipulation with Julia.
- **[Mastering Julia](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/mastering-julia/9781783553310/)** - *Malcolm Sherrington* - *2015* - Intermediate to advanced Julia: environment, tooling, and specialized topics.
- **[Statistics with Julia](https://www.springer.com/gp/book/9783030381404)** - *Hayden Klok & Yoni Nazarathy* - *2021* - Statistics and data science with Julia.
