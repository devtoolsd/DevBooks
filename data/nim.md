# Top Books on Nim

- **[Nim in Action](https://www.manning.com/books/nim-in-action)** - *Dominik Picheta* - *2017* - The primary Nim book by a core developer; language, Nimble, async, C/JS interop, metaprogramming, web.
- **[Computer Programming with the Nim Programming Language](https://nimprogrammingbook.com/book/nimprog.html)** - *Dr. Stefan Salewski* - *2023* - Free online introduction from basics to advanced Nim; gentle and comprehensive.
