# Top Books on System Design

- **[System Design Interview, Vol. 1, 2nd Ed.](https://www.amazon.com/System-Design-Interview-insiders-Second/dp/B08CMF2CQF)** - *Alex Xu* - *2020* - The most practical intro to system design with clear frameworks and real examples.
- **[System Design Interview, Vol. 2](https://www.amazon.com/System-Design-Interview-Insiders-Guide/dp/1736049119)** - *Alex Xu & Sahn Lam* - *2022* - Deeper dives: Google Maps, ad click aggregation, stock exchange, and distributed message queues.
- **[The System Design Primer](https://github.com/donnemartin/system-design-primer)** - *Donne Martin* - *2024* - Free, comprehensive GitHub guide covering scalability, reliability, and trade-off thinking.
- **[Designing Data-Intensive Applications](https://dataintensive.net/)** - *Martin Kleppmann* - *2017* - Foundation for system design: storage, streams, consistency, and real-world trade-offs.
- **[Building Microservices, 2nd Ed.](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/building-microservices-2nd/9781492034018/)** - *Sam Newman* - *2021* - Microservice design, integration, and deployment for scalable systems.
