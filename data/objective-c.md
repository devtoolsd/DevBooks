# Top Books on Objective-C

- **[Programming in Objective-C, 6th Ed.](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/programming-in-objective-c/9780133756937/)** - *Stephen G. Kochan* - *2014* - Concise tutorial for beginners; updated for modern OS X and iOS.
- **[Effective Objective-C 2.0](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/effective-objective-c-2-0/9780133386950/)** - *Matt Galloway* - *2013* - 52 specific ways to improve iOS and OS X programs; memory, protocols, ARC.
- **[Objective-C Programming: The Big Nerd Ranch Guide](https://www.bignerdranch.com/books/objective-c-programming-the-big-nerd-ranch-guide/)** - *Aaron Hillegass* - *2012* - Hands-on Objective-C and Cocoa from Big Nerd Ranch.
