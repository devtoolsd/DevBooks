# Top Books on Databases

- **[The Art of PostgreSQL, 2nd Ed.](https://theartofpostgresql.com/)** - *Dimitri Fontaine* - *2020* - Advanced SQL and PostgreSQL features through real application examples.
- **[PostgreSQL: Up and Running, 3rd Ed.](https://www.amazon.com/PostgreSQL-Running-Practical-Advanced-Database/dp/1491963417)** - *Regina Obe & Leo Hsu* - *2018* - A friendly, practical introduction to PostgreSQL for application developers.
- **[MongoDB: The Definitive Guide, 3rd Ed.](https://www.amazon.com/MongoDB-Definitive-Powerful-Scalable-Storage/dp/1491954469)** - *Shannon Bradshaw et al.* - *2019* - Comprehensive coverage of MongoDB for developers and operations.
- **[Redis in Action](https://www.manning.com/books/redis-in-action)** - *Josiah Carlson* - *2013* - Data structures, patterns, and real use cases for Redis beyond simple caching.
- **[Learning SQL, 4th Ed.](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/learning-sql-3rd/9781492057604/)** - *Alan Beaulieu* - *2023* - Window functions, materialized views, JSON; graded exercises for self-study.
- **[The Data Warehouse Toolkit, 3rd Ed.](https://www.amazon.com/Data-Warehouse-Toolkit-Definitive-Dimensional/dp/1118530802)** - *Ralph Kimball & Margy Ross* - *2013* - Foundational guide to dimensional modeling and analytical data design.
