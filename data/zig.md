# Top Books on Zig

- **[Learning Zig](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/learning-zig/9781835085127/)** - *Alex Rios* - *2025* - Zig fundamentals, safety, memory management, comptime, and more.
- **[Systems Programming with Zig](https://www.manning.com/books/systems-programming-with-zig)** - *Garrison Hinson-Hasty* - *2026* - Writing OS kernels, interpreters, and network services in Zig (MEAP).
- **[Zigbook](https://www.zigbook.net/)** - *Community* - *2024* - Free online resource: 62 chapters from basics to advanced; project-based.
