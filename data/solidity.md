# Top Books on Solidity

- **[Mastering Ethereum, 2nd Ed.](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/mastering-ethereum-2nd/9781098168414/)** - *Carlo Parisi, Alessandro Mazza, Niccolo Pozzolini* - *2025* - Ethereum internals, smart contracts, DApps, DeFi, security; Solidity and Vyper.
- **[Hands-On Smart Contract Development with Solidity and Ethereum](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/hands-on-smart-contract/9781492045250/)** - *Kevin Solorio, Randall Kanna, David H. Hoover* - *2019* - Smart contract fundamentals, Truffle, Web3, and security auditing.
- **[Solidity Programming Essentials, 2nd Ed.](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/solidity-programming-essentials/9781803231181/)** - *Ritesh Modi* - *2022* - Step-by-step Solidity: ERC20, NFTs, design patterns, and secure contracts.
- **[Mastering Ethereum](https://github.com/ethereumbook/ethereumbook)** - *Andreas M. Antonopoulos & Gavin Wood* - *2018* - Foundational Ethereum and smart contract development; free online.
