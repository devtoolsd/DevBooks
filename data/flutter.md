# Top Books on Flutter

- **[Flutter in Action](https://www.manning.com/books/flutter-in-action)** - *Eric Windmill* - *2018* - Cross-platform mobile development with Flutter and Dart.
- **[Practical Flutter](https://www.apress.com/gp/book/9781484249710)** - *Frank Zammetti* - *2019* - Build iOS and Android apps with Flutter; widgets, state, and APIs.
- **[Flutter Cookbook](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/flutter-cookbook/9781803245430/)** - *Simone Alessandria* - *2023* - Practical recipes for Flutter 3 and Dart 3; widgets, state, animations.
- **[Beginning Flutter](https://www.wiley.com/en-us/Beginning+Flutter%3A+A+Hands+On+Guide+To+App+Development-p-9781119550822)** - *Marco Napoli* - *2019* - Hands-on introduction to Flutter app development.
