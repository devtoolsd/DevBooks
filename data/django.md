# Top Books on Django

- **[Django for Professionals](https://www.amazon.com/Django-Professionals-Production-websites-Python/dp/1955390038)** - *William S. Vincent* - *2022* - Production-ready Django: deployment, security, and best practices.
- **[Django for APIs](https://djangoforapis.com/)** - *William S. Vincent* - *2022* - Build web APIs with Django REST Framework; free online.
- **[Web Development with Django, 2nd Ed.](https://www.packtpub.com/product/web-development-with-django-second-edition/9781803230603)** - *Ben Shaw et al.* - *2023* - Model-view-template, databases, CRUD, migrations, and practical projects.
- **[Django in Production](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/django-in-production/9781804610480/)** - *Arghya Saha* - *2024* - Deployment, performance, security, CI/CD, Docker, and AWS.
