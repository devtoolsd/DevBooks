# Top Books on Dart

- **[Flutter in Action](https://www.manning.com/books/flutter-in-action)** - *Eric Windmill* - *2018* - Cross-platform mobile development with Flutter and Dart; one codebase for iOS and Android.
- **[Flutter Cookbook, 2nd Ed.](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/flutter-cookbook/9781803245430/)** - *Simone Alessandria* - *2023* - Practical recipes for Flutter 3.10 and Dart 3; widgets, state, animations, ML, publishing.
- **[Flutter for Beginners, 2nd Ed.](https://www.packtpub.com/product/flutter-for-beginners-second-edition/9781800565999)** - *Alessandro Biessek* - *2021* - Introductory guide to Flutter and Dart: UI, navigation, animations, and release.
- **[Dart Apprentice](https://www.raywenderlich.com/books/dart-apprentice)** - *Ray Wenderlich Team* - *2022* - Learn Dart fundamentals for Flutter and beyond.
