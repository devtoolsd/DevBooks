# Top Books on Ada

- **[Programming in Ada 2022](https://www.cambridge.org/core/books/programming-in-ada-2022/144FA52E3F0C1F260CBF9DECD10A68C9)** - *John Barnes* - *2024* - Definitive reference for Ada 2022; program proof, multicore, standard library; by the language expert.
- **[Programming in Ada 2012 with a Preview of Ada 2022](https://www.cambridge.org/core/books/programming-in-ada-2012-with-a-preview-of-ada-2022/55B998B4E908A017E4D74A87364FF224)** - *John Barnes* - *2022* - Comprehensive Ada 2012 with a look ahead at Ada 2022.
- **[Introducing Ada 2012](https://www.ada-europe.org/manuals/Introducing_Ada_2012.pdf)** - *John Barnes* - *2012* - Concise overview of Ada 2012 for experienced programmers; free PDF.
