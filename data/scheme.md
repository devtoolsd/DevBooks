# Top Books on Scheme

- **[Structure and Interpretation of Computer Programs (SICP)](https://mitpress.mit.edu/9780262543231/structure-and-interpretation-of-computer-programs/)** - *Abelson, Sussman, Sussman* - *1996* - The classic; recursion, abstraction, and language design using Scheme; free online.
- **[The Scheme Programming Language, 4th Ed.](https://scheme.com/tspl4/)** - *R. Kent Dybvig* - *2009* - The definitive reference for standard Scheme; free online.
- **[How to Design Programs, 2nd Ed.](https://htdp.org/2023-9-14/Book/)** - *Felleisen, Findler, Flatt, Krishnamurthi* - *2018* - Program design using Scheme; free online.
- **[The Little Schemer](https://www.amazon.com/Little-Schemer-Daniel-P-Friedman/dp/0262560992)** - *Daniel P. Friedman & Matthias Felleisen* - *1995* - Recursion and Scheme through dialogue; gentle introduction.
